About Us

Learning the craftsmanship from local craftsmen, both Mounaz and Aya were determined to make it in the fashion industry, even if it meant going to workshops and swotting everything on their own. Mounaz’s love for jewelry and handbags has pushed her to integrate both worlds to create small, occasion bags. Aya’s toned-down minimalistic aesthetic balances her sister’s love for loud and embellished jewelry

Vision

OKHTEIN is a fashion brand that was founded by two Egyptian sisters, Aya and Mounaz, who are passionate about recreating and telling the stories of art and history through unique, handmade pieces. The brand is dedicated to redefining the modern Arab woman and celebrating female Arab empowerment and heritage while breaking global stereotypes. OKHTEIN's philosophy is centered around duality, family connections, and unity, which enable the creation of amazing opportunities. The brand believes that the best luxuries in life are those that can be shared.
